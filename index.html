Sacrifice

This game is based off of the religious laws of Ancient Greek Paganism, specifically the requirement that sacrifices be made to the Gods. The law dictates that people shall give sacrifices to the Gods, sometimes in small things like fruit or wine, but other times animal sacrifices would be required. These sacrifices often took place during celebrations in the Gods honor. These sacrifices appease the Gods by demonstrating the devoutness and loyalty of their followers, the more pleased the Gods the greater the rewards.

The game would begin with Hermes, the messenger God, appearing the tell the player (an Ancient Greek woman) that the Gods are displeased with her city's lack of piousness. He declares that if the player cannot appease the Gods with sacrifices within the next three days their city will be destroyed by Zeus. The player would collect items and deliver them to an altar, either appeasing the Gods or incurring their wrath.

The Player

This woman is you, it is the form you will embody while traversing the Ancient Greek city.

Hermes

The ancient Greek Messenger God who tells you of your city's plight so you may have a chance to save it.

Zeus

The King of the Gods, incredibly powerful, it is he who will destroy your city if you do not make the necessary sacrifices.

Goat

This animal is a possible sacrifice.

Pomegranates

A favorites of Hades, these fruits can be an offering.

Baby

This is your youngest child, an infant. Your husband is fighting in a war so you are alone with your newborn.
